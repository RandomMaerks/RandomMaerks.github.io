ABOUT


I'm some random bloke living in Vietnam, doing what I love best: type design & music.

I design typefaces for fun, and have been doing so since 2021. One of the typefaces is the exact one you are reading in at this moment: Overused Grotesk.

I also compose music that fits my tasteless ears which are mainly classical-esque style pieces for the piano.

I can speak Vietnamese, which is my native language, and English. I am also learning French just for the fun of it.

And yes, my coding skills are quite terrible, as you can see from this website which I have poorly constructed. It might break some time in the future, it might not. We'll see.
