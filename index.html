— About —

Thanks for visiting my website!

I am a Vietnamese type designer who goes by the name of RandomMaerks (don't ask why).

My goal with type design has been and will always be to provide cool-looking fonts (of somewhat decent quality) for people on the Internet to use, regardless if you are a fellow amateur designer with no budget or a high-profile company whose net worth is in the gazillions.

Most of my fonts are free to download and use, but you are looking to support me, you can send a donation or buy one of the non-free font packages that I offer on my store!
