\Random Typeface Assortment

Ultramono is a wide, geometric, and stylishly beautiful monospaced typeface. Each letter is carefully designed to grab the viewers' attention and make the design stands out.

"Ultramono" is perfect for posters, logos, headings, titles, etc. Lighter weights can also be used for small blocks of text, although some adjustments might be needed for legibility improvement.
